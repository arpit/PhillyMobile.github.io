PhillyMobile

A collaborative effort from Philly CocoaHeads and the Android Alliance, PhillyMobile is a one-night event that brings together developers from both Apple and Android backgrounds to discuss the latest platform technologies as well as our shared concerns and excitements of the ever changing mobile industry.

We'll have a wide range of complimentary lightning talks and plenty of cocktail time to mingle with your fellow developers.
